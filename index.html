Our story

Built for local communities

Roam exists to bridge the gap between people and the places around them — making local areas more connected, more discoverable, and more rewarding for everyone.

Our mission
Helping people become locals, everywhere

Roam sits at the intersection of local discovery, digital high street infrastructure, and community engagement. We believe local businesses are the heartbeat of every community — and they deserve the same digital tools as the big players.

We built Roam to create mutual value: the more businesses activate and share content, the more useful the app becomes for explorers. The more people explore, the more valuable Roam is for businesses. Everyone wins.

“
Roam helps people discover more from the places around them, while helping local businesses become more visible, more connected, and better equipped to grow.
— The Roam mission
What we stand for
Our values
01
Community first

We build for real people in real places. Every feature is designed to make local communities stronger and more rewarding to be part of.

02

03
