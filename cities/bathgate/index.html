A little history

Bathgate's James 'Paraffin' Young pioneered the oil industry by extracting paraffin from shale here in the 1850s — decades before the Texas oil boom. The shale bings still dot the West Lothian landscape.

📍 Local tip

The shale bings — pink and purple waste heaps from the oil industry — are unique in Scotland. Beecraigs Country Park for walking.
