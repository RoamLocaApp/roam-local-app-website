A little history

Coatbridge was the heart of Scotland's iron industry in the 19th century. Summerlee Museum preserves the extraordinary industrial heritage on the original ironworks site.

📍 Local tip

Summerlee Museum is genuinely excellent and free — working tramways, miners' cottages, and extraordinary industrial machinery.
